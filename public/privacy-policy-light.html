HariMarkify Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 4, 2026

At HariMarkify, we prioritize the privacy and security of our clients, website visitors, and partners. This Privacy Policy details how we collect, store, share, and protect your information when you visit our website, contact us via WhatsApp, or collaborate on digital marketing campaigns.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when submitting inquiries through our contact forms or starting chats on WhatsApp. This includes:

  • Contact Details (Name, Phone number, Email address, WhatsApp contact info).
  • Business details (Business name, Niche industry, Website URL, current ad budgets).
  • Any message details or files you attach to service request forms.

2. How We Use Your Data

We process collected information to fulfill requests and support business optimization strategies, specifically:

  • Delivering free marketing audits and campaign consultation insights.
  • Establishing project setup configurations, tracking pixels, and mapping local schemas.
  • Corresponding directly regarding performance statistics, monthly growth metrics, and ad optimizations.
  • Complying with applicable legal processes and security frameworks.

3. Data Protection and Security

We employ strict security measures (SSL certification, limited team access, and encrypted local configurations) to ensure that your business and campaign stats are protected. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third-party brokers.

4. Cookies and Analytical Tracking

Our website uses basic Google Analytics tracking to evaluate page views, loading times, and viewport metrics. You can choose to disable browser cookies in your device settings without affecting your access to our core services.

5. Contact and Opt-Out Rights

If you have questions about this policy, want to request deletion of your contact files, or wish to opt-out of notifications, please contact us directly via WhatsApp or call us at +91 9441745745.
